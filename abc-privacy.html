Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 04, 2026

1. Introduction

ABC Touch & Move is committed to protecting the privacy of your child. We comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and Apple's Kids Category guidelines.

2. No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from children. We do not require registration to use the app, and we do not track or share your child's data with third parties.

3. Usage Analytics

We may collect limited, non-personal usage data (such as which letters are played most) to improve the educational experience, but this is never linked to any user identity.

4. Advertising

ABC Touch & Move contains NO third-party advertising. This ensures a safe, uninterrupted learning environment for your child.

5. External Links

External links (to support or these legal terms) are strictly limited and protected by a "Parental Gate".

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@abctouchmove.com.

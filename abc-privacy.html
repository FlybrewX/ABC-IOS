Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 04, 2026

1. Commitment to Privacy

ABC Touch & Move is a children's educational app. We take privacy very seriously and comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and Apple's strict Kids Category guidelines.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information from you or your child. We do not require account creation, email addresses, or any identification to use the app.

3. No Subscriptions & One-Time Payment

The app offers a free trial of 3 games. A one-time purchase is available to unlock the full version. **We do not have recurring subscriptions**, which means we never collect payment information or billing details. All purchases are handled securely by Apple.

4. No Third-Party Analytics or Ads

We do not use any third-party analytics (like Google Analytics or Facebook) that could track your child. There are NO ads in this app.

5. Safe Parental Gate

All external communication (like support emails or legal links) is protected by a Parental Gate to ensure your child remains safely within the app.

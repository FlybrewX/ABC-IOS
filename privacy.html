Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 04, 2026

1. Kid-Safe & COPPA Compliant

ABC Touch & Move is built for children. We strictly follow the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple’s Kids Category guidelines. Our app is a safe digital playground for early learners.

2. Zero Personal Data Collection

We believe in absolute privacy. Our app:

  • Does NOT collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • Does NOT track your location.
  • Does NOT require account registration.
  • Does NOT access your camera or microphone.

3. No Ads & No Third-Party Tracking

We do not include any advertisements, and we do not use third-party analytics (like Google or Facebook) that could track your child's behavior across other apps or websites.

4. Secure Parental Gate

All external links (like support emails or legal pages) and the purchase screen are protected by a Parental Gate. This requires a parent to solve a math problem, ensuring children cannot leave the app or make purchases accidentally.

5. One-Time Purchase Security

All financial transactions are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. We never see or store your credit card or billing information.
